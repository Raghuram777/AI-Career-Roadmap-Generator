## üìã Q&A Preparation

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why use multiple AI agents instead of one?**  
A: Specialized agents perform better than generalists. Each agent has specific expertise, leading to higher quality outputs and better user experience.

**Q: How do you handle AI hallucinations?**  
A: We use prompt engineering, vector database for grounding, and validation layers. All interview questions are pre-verified.

**Q: What's the cost of running AI operations?**  
A: Current estimate: $0.50-1.50 per user per month. This decreases with caching and optimization.

**Q: How do you scale to millions of users?**  
A: Microservices architecture, horizontal scaling, caching layers, and async processing for AI tasks.

### Business Questions

**Q: What's your revenue model?**  
A: Freemium: Basic roadmaps free, Premium ($15/mo) for AI mentor and interviews, Enterprise (custom pricing) for companies.

**Q: Who are your target users?**  
A: Primary: College students and recent graduates. Secondary: Career switchers (25-35 age group).

**Q: How do you acquire users?**  
A: Content marketing, SEO, partnerships with coding bootcamps, college programs, and social media.

**Q: What's your competitive advantage?**  
A: AI agents + adaptation + interview focus. No competitor has all three.
